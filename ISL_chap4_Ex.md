need\ to\ show:
\\y=\frac{x}{1+x}\ (4.2)
\Leftrightarrow
\frac{y}{1-y}=x\ \ (4.3)
\\i.e.\ that: 1+x = \frac{1}{1-y}
\\
\\
\begin{align}
y &= \frac{x}{1+x}
\\y + yx &= x
\\-xy-y+x&=0
\\1-xy-y+x&=1
\\(1-y)(1+x)&=1
\\1+x&=\frac{1}{1-y}
\end{align}
\)
